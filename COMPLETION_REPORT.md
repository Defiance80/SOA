## LESSONS LEARNED

### What Worked Well

1. **Systematic approach** - Chapters 1-10 first (illustrated), then 11-18 (adaptation)
2. **Bible canon as north star** - Clear guidelines prevented drift
3. **Enhancement notes per chapter** - Easy to track what changed
4. **Hyphen removal rule** - Improved natural flow significantly
5. **15-20% trim target** - Consistent quality without losing substance

### Challenges Overcome

1. **Chapter 11 full rewrite** - Original didn't match Bible canon, rebuilt from scratch
2. **Eryndor thread** - Retroactively added setup in Ch. 3, payoff in Ch. 14
3. **Nidus complexity** - Shifted from villain to tragic survivor mid-project
4. **Time management** - Split across 2 sessions, maintained momentum

### Best Practices Established

- **Read → Enhance → Push immediately** - Don't batch commits
- **Detailed commit messages** - Future-you will thank past-you
- **Canon check before AND after** - Bible reference throughout
- **Emotional beats first, prose second** - Preserve feeling, then tighten words
- **Character voice consistency** - Each person has distinct speech patterns
